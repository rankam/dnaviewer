Welcome to DNA Viewer

Using DNA Viewer, you can explore the DNA features of a DNA molecule. To your left, you will find a Plasmid Diagram that visually shows the features of the DNA molecule. Clicking on one of the DNA features in the Plasmid Diagram will replace this text with more information about the feature such as its sequence and direction.
